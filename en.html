In 3 steps to accident processing

damage report

Simply report your vehicle damage digitally using our contact form and we will get in touch with you immediately - or call our hotline directly. If you are injured, the liability insurance will cover the costs

order processing

Once we have all the information about your car accident, we will immediately take further steps to ensure the fastest possible claims settlement. This includes an independent vehicle appraiser, experienced legal counsel or a replacement vehicle for your vehicle appraisal, as required

assumption of costs

You can have the amount of damage paid out to you. Alternatively, you have the option of handing in your damaged vehicle to the workshop of your choice. The legal adviser will take care of the assumption of costs by the insurance company. If you do not know a specialist workshop, we will be happy to refer you to a specialist workshop
